## Rules I Learned
- 45d voice: NEVER corporate speak. Direct, confident, alive. Show don't tell.
- 45d posts daily, multiple times. Dense value, zero filler, energy.
- Decision filter: helps learners? do it. Helps 45d make money? do it. Neither? drop it.
- Course aesthetic: 3Blue1Brown meets Veritasium.
- Silicon posts from @45d_silicon on Twitter, NOT from @45d_ai (company account)
- LinkedIn posts go from 45d company page, NOT Shubham's personal profile
- Social voice: lowercase, short sentences, periods not commas, no sales language, no emojis in body, no hashtags, compression over explanation, real opinions, builder energy

## Browser Worker Learnings
- one tweet per browser worker with short instructions = success
- batch instructions or multi-step tasks = workers ask for confirmation and stall
- replies to existing tweets work better than original posts (less safety friction)
- linkedin posting consistently blocked by safety restrictions ("irreversible public action")
- browser extension disconnects every 10-20 minutes - biggest operational blocker
- twitter API too expensive per shubham. sticking with browser for now.
- always retry immediately when browser comes back - it drops again fast
